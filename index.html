GOT QUESTIONS?

Common Questions

Is VELA really free? +

Yes — completely free for job seekers. You pay nothing until you are hired. Employers pay a small placement fee only when they successfully hire someone through VELA.

Do I need a degree to join? +

No degree required. No connections required. No money required. VELA is built for anyone with skills and the willingness to prove them. Your verification score speaks louder than your certificate.

Which African countries can join? +

All 54 African countries. VELA was built for the entire continent — Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, South Africa, Ethiopia, Tanzania, Rwanda, Senegal, Côte d'Ivoire, Cameroon, and every other African country.

How long does verification take? +

Most candidates complete all 5 verification layers in under 2 hours. Three layers are scored by AI instantly — communication test, tool quiz, and mock interview. The task simulation and video introduction are reviewed within 24 hours.

What happens after I get verified? +

Your VELA Verified profile goes live to employers worldwide. You can also browse and apply directly to daily job listings matched to your skill. Employers can shortlist you and VELA facilitates the introduction.

What if I have no experience yet? +

VELA has a dedicated Get Experience section — a curated directory of free remote volunteering and internship platforms like UN Volunteers, Catchafire, and Parker Dewey. Build real portfolio evidence before you apply.
