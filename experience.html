Make it count
How to turn experience into a VELA portfolio
01
Document everything. Screenshot your work, save outputs, record outcomes. Numbers matter — "grew nonprofit's Instagram by 340 followers in 4 weeks" beats "managed social media".
02
Ask for a reference letter. After any volunteer or internship project, ask the organisation for a short written reference. Even one paragraph. Employers value this.
03
Add it to your VELA portfolio. Go to your VELA portfolio builder, add a project card for each experience, upload your outputs or screenshots, and write what you achieved.
04
Start with Forage first. Complete at least one Forage programme before applying anywhere. It takes 3–6 hours and gives you a certificate from a top company with zero experience required.
05
Pick platforms that match your skill. Don't apply randomly. Pick the platform where your skill category is strongest. Catchafire for social media and data. UN Volunteers for research and translation.
06
Do 2–3 projects then apply for jobs. Two or three completed projects with documented results is enough portfolio evidence to apply for entry-level remote roles on VELA's jobs board.
